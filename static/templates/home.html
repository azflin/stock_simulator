World's simplest and fastest stock simulator.

Build portfolios and trade securities in real time powered by Yahoo Finance.

Register

Rules/Help:

  • All portfolios start with $100,000. You can create an unlimited number of portfolios.
  • Valid securities you can buy are tickers appearing on Yahoo Finance without punctuation. This limits you to mostly U.S. securities.
  • Shorting IS allowed on all securities. To short, place a "Sell" transaction of a security you don't own.
    • Your total equity must be > 150% of your short exposure. With $100,000 in equity, your maximum short exposure is $200,000.
  • Currently no dividends are being paid out on any securities that pay dividends.
